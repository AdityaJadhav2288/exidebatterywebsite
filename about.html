About Exide Battery Shop

Exide Battery Shop has been a trusted leader in the battery industry for over 70 years. We specialize in providing high-quality, reliable, and innovative energy storage solutions for both individual consumers and industries.

From automotive to industrial to inverter batteries, Exide is synonymous with performance and durability. Our dedication to superior technology and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for millions of customers worldwide.

We believe in empowering our customers with energy solutions that not only meet their needs but also exceed expectations. Our expert team is committed to delivering products that keep you charged and ready, no matter the situation.

Our Mission

To deliver cutting-edge, sustainable energy storage solutions that drive progress for individuals, businesses, and industries around the world.

Our Journey

  • 1950 - Exide Battery Shop was founded, starting with automotive batteries.
  • 1975 - Expansion into industrial battery solutions, providing backup power to major industries.
  • 1990 - Introduced our first line of inverter batteries, revolutionizing home energy storage.
  • 2020 - Celebrated 70 years of innovation, delivering energy solutions to over 10 million customers worldwide.
